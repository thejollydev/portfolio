About Me

I've been running Linux daily since I was 15. What started with building my first PC at 11 and teaching myself C++ from a library book became a career-long obsession with how systems work at every layer.

I spent a decade in operations — automotive finance management and hotel operations — where I developed a deep instinct for reliability, incident response, and systematic problem-solving. That same mindset now drives how I build and operate infrastructure.

In 2025 I designed and deployed BezaForge: a production private cloud built from hardware selection through application deployment. Proxmox hypervisor, GPU passthrough, 5-VLAN network, 10+ Docker services, full observability stack. Everything documented, monitored, and running.

I hold an IBM Back-End Development Professional Certificate and PCEP Python certification. I'm completing my BS in Computer Science and actively pursuing Google Cloud Professional Architect certification. I learn by building things that actually run in production.
